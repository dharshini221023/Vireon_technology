Our Story

Built on Purpose and Precision

Vireon Technologies was founded in 2014 by a team of seasoned engineers and technology strategists who believed that enterprise software could — and should — be better. Starting with a small team of 12 in Singapore, we grew organically by solving complex problems for clients who demanded excellence.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of companies does Vireon typically work with?
Vireon works with mid-market and enterprise companies across 10+ industries. Our typical client has 500+ employees, operates across multiple geographies, and is facing a complex technology challenge that requires both strategic thinking and deep technical execution.
How long does a typical engagement take?
Engagement length varies by scope. A discovery and architecture review typically takes 4–6 weeks. A full enterprise platform build ranges from 4 to 18 months. We also offer ongoing managed services and consulting retainers for clients who prefer continuous partnership.
Do you work with fixed-price or time-and-materials contracts?
We offer both engagement models. For well-defined scopes, fixed-price gives clients budget certainty. For evolving requirements or agile development, time-and-materials with monthly caps provides the flexibility to adapt. We recommend the model that best suits your project's nature.
What certifications does Vireon hold?
Vireon is ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II certified for information security. Our engineers hold AWS Certified Solutions Architect Professional, Google Professional Cloud Architect, Microsoft Azure Expert, and numerous specialty certifications. We're an AWS Advanced Tier Partner and Microsoft Gold Partner.
How do you handle data privacy and intellectual property?
All client data and IP belongs exclusively to the client. We operate under strict NDAs and data processing agreements aligned with GDPR, PDPA (Singapore), and other applicable regulations. Source code, designs, and deliverables are fully transferred to clients upon project completion.
